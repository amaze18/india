Anupam Purwar

admin | January 3, 2022

Anupam is working as a Research Scientist with Amazon developing tech products for Amazon’s global network. He specializes in solving Machine Learning and Operations Research problems. Previously, he was credited with developing Railways as the third mode of transport for Amazon which fetched him two awards from Global VP. Prior to this, Anupam worked as a Research Scientist at Indian Institute of Science (IISc). At IISc, he was part of a multi-institutional effort which included IITs and ISRO to develop ultra-high-temperature materials and novel structures, which resulted in 25+ peer reviewed publications with Anupam as the lead author. He graduated among the top 10% of the class from Indian School of Business and BITS Pilani. He is passionate about contributing towards the education of kids from underprivileged backgrounds. He practises sustainability, takes pride in cycling every day to commute and likes mentoring startups.
